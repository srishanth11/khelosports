Meet the Coach

Imran

NIS graduate (2024–2025)
Co-Founder

Imran, our visionary Co-Founder and a certified NIS graduate, founded Khelo Sports with a mission to bring joy, health, and a sense of community to children's lives through sports. With a strong background in youth engagement and sports education, Imran ensures that every child grows in a safe, energetic, and fun-filled environment. Notably, Imran has represented Telangana in the National Handball Championship, showcasing his dedication and excellence in the sport at the highest level.

B. Pawan Kumar

B.Com graduate
Head Coach

Pawan Kumar, our dynamic Head Coach, leads with passion and unmatched enthusiasm. He designs inclusive and engaging programs that cater to the unique strengths of every child, making fitness both fun and empowering. Pawan has proudly represented Telangana in the National Handball Championship, bringing his on-ground experience and competitive spirit to every training session.

Tejaswini

B.Com graduate
Assistant Coach

Tejaswini is a driving force behind Khelo Sports’ creative and inclusive approach. As Co-Founder, she works closely with children to develop programs that foster confidence, physical fitness, and joy. A state-level handball player, Tejaswini has represented Telangana in the National Handball Championship, reflecting her deep commitment to sports and child development.

Sultan Haseeb Uddin Ahmed

Sultan Haseeb brings strategic insight and a heart for sports to Khelo Sports. His programs are thoughtfully crafted to encourage teamwork, discipline, and personal growth.
