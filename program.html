Meet the Team

CEO - Srishanth Kumar

Srishanth Kumar, our visionary CEO, founded Khelo Sports with a mission to bring joy, health, and community to children's lives through sports. With a passion for fitness and years of experience in youth engagement, he ensures every child experiences fun and growth in a safe environment.

Co-founder - Ananya Singh

Ananya Singh brings unmatched energy and creativity to Khelo Sports. As Co-founder, she designs inclusive programs that cater to every child’s unique strengths, making fitness fun, accessible, and confidence-boosting for all.
