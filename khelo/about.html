About Us

Welcome to Khelo Sports fitness camp we focus on promoting health and wellness through engaging fitness activities for children. Our fun programs encourage physical activity, teamwork, and confidence, all while fostering a sense of community. Led by certified instructors, we provide a safe and enjoyable environment where kids can thrive. Join us to help your child develop a lifelong love for fitness!

Our Mission

To inspire children to embrace an active lifestyle through fun, supportive, and skill-building sports programs.
